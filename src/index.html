MARBL Finding Aids

    EUCLID Irish Literary Collections Portal MARBL Subject Guides Digital Collections

Archives create guides called finding aids to help researchers use manuscript collections. These collections, which contain material related to a person, family, organization, or subject, may encompass a wide variety of materials including correspondence, photographs, films, audio recordings, diaries, manuscripts, printed material, and maps. Finding aids generally include a description of the collection and the person or organization that created it along with a detailed inventory or list of contents. Please note that some collections may not yet have finding aids available and that this site is a work in progress.
