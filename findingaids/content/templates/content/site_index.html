About EmoryFindingAids

The EmoryFindingAids database provides centralized access to detailed descriptions of archival and manuscript collections held in various repositories at Emory.  Finding aids describe our unique primary resources and help researchers determine if collections contain material of interest to them and exactly where that material is located. The database includes finding aids for both processed and unprocessed collections.  At present, the database contains finding aids for MARBL, the Emory University Archives, and the Emory Law School Archives.  Additional Emory repositories will be added over time.
