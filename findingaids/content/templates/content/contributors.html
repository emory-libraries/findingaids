The EmoryFindingAids database provides centralized access to detailed descriptions of archival and manuscript collections held in various repositories at Emory University. As this project is a "work in progress," new finding aids are being created and added to the database all the time.

The list below includes the names of the repositories that are currently participating in the EmoryFindingAids database. Their websites will provide more details regarding collection scope, operating hours, and other helpful information.

  • Stuart A. Rose Manuscript, Archives, and Rare Book Library
  • Emory University Archives
  • Emory Law School Archives
  • Pitts Theology Library
  • Woodruff Health Sciences Center Library
  • Oxford College Archives
