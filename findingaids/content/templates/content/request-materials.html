The Stuart A. Rose Manuscript, Archives, and Rare Book Library, which includes the Emory University Archives, is located in the Robert W. Woodruff Library on Emory University's main Atlanta campus. It is a closed-stacks library which means that all of its holdings are located in secure areas and are accessible in our on-site reading room. Please visit the Rose Library website to register and request materials. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact the Rose Library reference staff.

For access to collections at other campus repositories, please contact the archivist for that library directly:
